Tour Guides

Explore the hidden gems of Greece with our expert tour guides:

Milos: Visit the Plaka Castle, Milos Mining Museum, Tsigrado Beach, Sarakiniko Beach, and Papafragas Cave.

Symi: Discover Nanou Beach, Marathounda Beach, take a boat ride to Agios Georgios Dysalona Beach, explore Kali Strata, and visit Panormitis Monastery.

Athens: Experience the Monastiraki Flea Market, Areopagus, Bobo Winebar, Benaki Museum, and Philopappos Hill.

Travel Tips

  • Carry light luggage due to a lot of walking.
  • You will not need to book a rental car ahead of time.
  • Do not bring a lot of cash; use ATMs when you get there.
  • Give yourself a day between your ferry and flight home because ferries are not always on time.
  • Location matters! It is hard to find restaurants outside of main cities and resorts/hotels.
